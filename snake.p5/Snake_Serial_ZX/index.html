Controls

Connect Serial: Click button to connect to Arduino

WASD: Move snake

R: Reset | P/O: Speed up/down

F/Space: Toggle fullscreen

Z/X: Turn left/right

1-9: 1: Up, 2: Left, 3: Down, 4: Right, 5: Reset, 6: Speed down, 7: Speed up, 8: Turn left, 9: Turn right

Note: WebSerial requires Chrome/Edge browser
